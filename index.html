Do I really pay only once with no renewal fees?
Yes, absolutely. 4POS is a once-off purchase per PC licence. There are no monthly or yearly fees. Future upgrades are free for the lifetime of your licence.
What hardware do I need to run 4POS?
Any Windows 7 SP1 or newer PC with at least 4GB RAM works perfectly. The database is compact — any hard drive with 20GB+ free space is sufficient for years of data.
Can I network multiple tills together?
Yes. 4POS is fully networkable. Our largest current site runs 26 stations from a single back office. Each station needs its own licence; setup across a local network takes minutes.
How easy is switching from my current POS system?
Very easy. Thousands of businesses have migrated to 4POS. Export your product list to Excel and import it directly. We assist with data migration at no extra charge.
What support options are available?
600+ page manual, 150+ YouTube training videos, a full knowledge base, free WhatsApp and email support, and a free remote installation session for your first PC. Extended phone and TeamViewer contracts are also available.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes! Download 4POS and enjoy a full-featured 20-day free trial — no credit card required. All features are unlocked so you can test everything before you buy.
